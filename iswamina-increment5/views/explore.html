Founded in 2025 in the Oakland district of Pittsburgh.

Serving intellectually curious students, artists, technologists, and visitors eager to explore how creativity and innovation shape the future of artistic expression.

Mission Statement

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the powerful intersection of art, technology, and human imagination. Through immersive exhibitions, experimental digital installations, and interactive experiences, the museum invites visitors to engage with art in entirely new ways. MonoMuse encourages curiosity, innovation, and creative exploration by showcasing how emerging technologies are transforming the way art is created, shared, and experienced across the world.

Milestones

2025

MonoMuse officially opens in the Oakland district of Pittsburgh, introducing visitors to groundbreaking digital installations and immersive exhibitions that explore the evolving relationship between technology and creativity.

Spring 2026

Light & Code exhibition debuts. This exhibition explores how artists combine programming, projection mapping, and generative algorithms to create living works of digital art. Visitors will experience interactive environments where light, code, and human movement shape the artwork in real time. Coming soon.

Fall 2026

Human + Machine exhibition launches, examining the growing collaboration between artists and artificial intelligence. This exhibition will feature AI-generated paintings, machine-assisted music, and installations that allow visitors to participate in the creative process alongside intelligent algorithms. Coming soon.

2027

MonoMuse plans to open the Immersive Media Lab, a groundbreaking space dedicated to virtual reality, augmented reality, and interactive storytelling. Visitors will be able to step into digital worlds, experiment with creative technologies, and experience the future of immersive artistic expression. Coming soon.
